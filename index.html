Made for More is a Christian Charity located in Northern Ireland. The organisation was founded in 2018 by a local teacher called Jamie Meaklim who over 8 years within the education system, noticed a concerning trends developing in the areas of mental health, self-esteem, alcohol & drug dependency amongst secondary school pupils & the need for an organisation to meet the need in this area.

Our work is primarily delivered across 3 specific groups: Schools, Youth Organisations & Churches in order to reach a wide demographic of young people via face to face workshops & e-learning and video series. Our aim is to engage a generation of young people in schools, churches, youth organisations and communities to educate and equip them with the knowledge needed to value who they are and help them make positive decisions for a more purposeful future.
